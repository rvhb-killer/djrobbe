01 — AI Scanning

Iris works
for you, 24/7.

IRIS continuously scrapes the internet — from trade journals and government reports to academic research and social media. The AI filters out noise and surfaces only the signals that are relevant to your sector and region.

You choose the geographic region to search: local, European or global. Your own datasets and sources can be added easily.

  • ✓ Region selection — search locally, nationally or globally
  • ✓ Custom domains — configure your own sources and feeds
  • ✓ Multilingual — NL, EN and FR sources scanned in parallel
  • ✓ Custom datasets — upload internal documents as context
  • ✓ Automatic deduplication — every story only appears once

02 — PESTEL Trend Radar

Signals organized
across six domains.

Every scanned signal is automatically classified according to the PESTEL model — Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental and Legal. On the trend radar, you can see at a glance where pressure is building and over which time horizon.

You configure which domains and subcategories are relevant to your organization. The radar is fully adaptable to your own foresight methodology.

  • ✓ Automatic PESTEL classification — AI categorizes every signal
  • ✓ Time dimension — from 0–2 years to 10+ years on the radar
  • ✓ Custom domains — add your own subcategories
  • ✓ Interactive — click on a dot to see the full signal
  • ✓ Exportable — radar as image or PDF

03 — 3 boxes model

Prioritize what truly
matters to your company.

Based on your internal documents and strategy, IRIS determines which scanned signals are most relevant to your organization. For this, we use the 3 Boxes method — a proven exercise from strategic innovation practice.

What did we miss?

Signals that had been emerging for some time but remained under the radar. Learn from blind spots.

Where do we dare to experiment?

Trends that are uncertain but offer opportunity. This is where you test with limited risk.

What do we see emerging?

Weak signals with strong future potential. These are your strategic levers.
