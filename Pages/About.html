Background

Boren of
TomorrowLab

IRIS is not a product out of nowhere. The platform grew out of years of foresight work. At TomorrowLab, one of the leading strategic innovation agencies in Belgium. There we learned how consultants really work: in fast sessions, with too many tabs open and too little time to fully grasp everything.

TomorrowLab helped large organizations such as multinationals, governments, and non-profits. That methodology systematically scanning, interpreting, and strategically translating. Forms the backbone of IRIS.

What used to take hours of manual work in workshops, IRIS now does in minutes. The same depth. The same rigor. But scalable for every team.
