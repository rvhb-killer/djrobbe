Veelgestelde vragen

Need a quick answer?

For which type of organizations is IRIS intended?

IRIS is built for strategic companies, innovation teams, and policymakers at medium to large organizations. Both private sector and government. From a team of 2 to a multi-business unit with dozens of users.

How long does the onboarding take?

Most teams are operational within one day. We personally guide every customer in setting up the scan profiles, PESTEL domains, and reporting templates.

Can I try IRIS before I decide?

Yes. You can start with a free sector scan that we perform for you. This way, you immediately see which signals IRIS picks up for your domain, without any obligation.

Is er een minimale contractduur?

Licenses run on an annual basis. For Enterprise customers, we will review the most suitable agreement together. Contact us for a personalized quote.

What about data privacy and security?

IRIS is fully GDPR compliant. Data is stored on European servers. Internal documents that you upload are never used for training AI models and are strictly separated by client.
